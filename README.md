# 📊 Employee Loyalty Analysis Dashboard (IBM HR Data)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive HR analytics dashboard built using IBM employee data.  
The goal is to analyze employee loyalty, understand attrition patterns, and identify key factors influencing workforce retention.

The dashboard transforms raw HR data into actionable insights to support better decision-making in human resource management.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition across different job roles and levels  
- Understand the impact of promotions on employee retention  
- Evaluate employee satisfaction and manager relationships  
- Explore salary distribution and compensation efficiency  
- Identify trends that affect employee loyalty  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- **Employee Distribution**
  - Count of employees by job role and job level  
  - Attrition comparison (Yes / No)

- **Career Growth Analysis**
  - TCC (career growth rate) by job role  
  - Years since last promotion analysis  

- **Employee Satisfaction**
  - Relationship satisfaction based on years with current manager  

- **Financial Insights**
  - Average yearly income  
  - RER (compensation efficiency ratio)

- **Workforce Trends**
  - Average years in current role  
  - Attrition patterns across departments  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** → Data visualization & dashboard creation  
- **Excel** → Data cleaning & preprocessing  
- **DAX** → Calculated measures and KPIs  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Higher attrition observed in roles like Sales Representatives  
- Long periods without promotion lead to lower satisfaction  
- Compensation and career growth strongly impact retention  
- Managerial roles show higher stability and longer tenure  

---

## 🚀 Impact
This dashboard helps HR teams:
- Detect high-risk attrition areas  
- Improve employee engagement strategies  
- Make data-driven decisions  
- Enhance workforce stability
